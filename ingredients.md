Ingredients which we need to make this dessert are as follows;

Maida           - 2 cups;
Milk powder     - 4 tbsp;
Sugar           - 1 1/2 cup;
Oil             - 4 tbsp;
Vanilla Essence - few drops;
Baking powder   - 3/4 tsp;
Baking soda     - 1/2 tsp;
Salt            - a pinch;
water           - 2 cups;
lemon juice     - 2 tsp;
